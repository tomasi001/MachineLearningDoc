Getting Started

We will be following TensorFlow's Learning path, which on their website, they say consists of:

  • Coding skills: Building ML models involves much more than just knowing ML concepts - it requires coding in order to do the data management, parameter tuning, and parsing results needed to test and optimise your model.

  • Math and stats: ML is a math heavy discipline, so if you plan to modify ML models or build new ones from scratch, familiarity with the underlying math concepts is crucial to the process.

  • ML theory: Knowing the basics of ML theory will give you a foundation to build on, and help you troubleshoot when something goes wrong.

  • Building your own projects: Getting hands on experience with ML is the best way to put your knowledge to the test, so don't be afraid to dive in early with a simple collaboration or tutorial to get some practice.

Rituals

START OF EACH STEP

We will meet to make sure everyone starts at the same time and answer any questions any members may have.


DURATION OF EACH STEP

We will create a step specific discussion topic in the slack channel where members can post in the thread, ask and answer FAQs and share any interesting finds.


END OF EACH STEP

We will meet to discuss what we have learned and do a shared learning / Q&A session


END OF EACH SECTION

We will have a mini-hackathon, depending on the size of the club this will consist of either one group or sub-divided groups to showcase the skills we have just learned.
